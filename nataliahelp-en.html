About the Project

The Mutual Support Platform was created for people who work hard but still cannot afford to buy a home on their own. Here, participants make small voluntary contributions — 10€, 50€, or 100€ — and the collected funds help people reach their housing goals: an apartment, a house, a plot of land, a cottage, or other housing-related needs.

After receiving support, participants remain in the project, helping the next ones. Everything is voluntary, transparent, and honest: funds movement and the participant queue are visible on the website.

Platform commission is small and used only for development: stable site operation, security, user support, and transparency tools.

How It Works

  1. Contribution. You make a voluntary contribution — 10€, 50€, or 100€.
  2. Queue. Funds are distributed through a transparent queue.
  3. Support. One participant receives support for their housing goal.
  4. Continuity. After receiving help, you stay and support the next participants.

Why It Works

Saving for a home alone can take years or may even be impossible. When people unite, small contributions create a real impact. Transparency, simplicity, and consistency make this system work.

Principles & Transparency

  • Housing only: apartment, house, land, cottage, or other housing-related goals.
  • Voluntary: no obligations, contributions are small and optional.
  • Transparent: queue and reports are visible on the website.
  • Responsible: commission is only for platform improvement.

FAQ

Voluntary: 10 €, 50 €, or 100 € — you choose.

What happens after I receive support?

You remain in the platform and continue helping others.

Where does the commission go?

Can I donate without joining the queue?

Yes! Anyone can make a donation and support participants.
