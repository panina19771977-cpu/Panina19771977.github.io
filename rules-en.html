Participation Terms

The Mutual Support Platform is built on honesty, transparency, and voluntary participation. Below are the principles and rules that ensure the stability and fairness of the platform for all participants.

1. Voluntary Participation

Everyone joins the platform voluntarily. There are no contracts, no binding obligations, and no forced payments.

2. Contribution Amounts

Participants make voluntary contributions of 10€, 50€, or 100€. You decide how much and how often to contribute.

3. Purpose of the Platform

The platform exists solely to support housing-related goals:

  • apartment
  • house
  • land
  • summer cottage
  • renovation
  • mortgage down payment

Medical expenses, business funding, vehicles, investments, and unrelated goals are not included.

4. Queue System

All participants enter a transparent queue. Support is distributed according to:

  • the date of application,
  • confirmed participation,
  • activity and regularity of contributions,
  • overall platform movement.

5. Staying After Receiving Support

Participants who receive support remain in the platform to help others. This is the foundation of the mutual support system.

6. Donation Option

Anyone may make a donation via PayPal or Revolut without joining the queue. Donations accelerate the movement of the platform and help other participants.

7. Transparency

The following information is publicly available:

  • participant queue,
  • fund movement,
  • issued support,
  • platform commission usage.

8. Commission

The platform retains a small commission used exclusively for:

  • hosting and domain costs,
  • technical support,
  • site maintenance and development,
  • security and data protection.

9. Freedom to Leave

Participants may leave the platform at any time. All activity is optional and voluntary.

10. Age Requirement

Participation is allowed for adults aged 18 and older.

11. No Guarantees of Timing

The platform does not promise exact dates for receiving support. The speed depends on the number of participants, contributions, and donations.

12. Honesty and Respect

Participants must provide truthful information and respect others. The platform is built on mutual trust.
